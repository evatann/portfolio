Project Ethelontêr XXI – Freshmen Orientation

Mar 2025 – Jul 2025

Overview

As part of the NUS Community Service Club’s (CSC) flagship initiatives, Project Ethelontêr XXI – Freshmen Volunteerism Orientation Camp was organised to welcome incoming NUS students and inspire meaningful community service. The 4-day camp brought together freshmen, senior student leaders, and partner organisations to foster empathy, teamwork, and social responsibility while supporting underprivileged groups in Singapore.

In my role as Head of Logistics and Marketing, I oversaw end-to-end planning and execution of sponsorships, procurement, and event operations. This included drafting sponsorship proposals and outreach materials, securing in-kind and monetary contributions from corporate partners, coordinating procurement and timely delivery of camp resources, managing equipment and manpower, and liaising with vendors for production of shirts and tote bags.

Additionally, I facilitated on-ground activities by assisting freshmen participants during MINDS sessions, transporting and setting up materials, coordinating activity stations, and engaging directly with beneficiaries through games and clay sculpting. This experience strengthened my leadership, stakeholder management, event coordination, and volunteer facilitation skills, while deepening my empathy and understanding of individuals with intellectual disabilities.

Key Highlights

➤Leadership Role: Served as Head of Logistics and Marketing, managing sponsorships, procurement, and event operations.
➤Event Coordination: Oversaw equipment, manpower, and vendor management to ensure smooth execution of a 4-day volunteer camp.
➤Volunteer Engagement: Assisted freshmen participants during MINDS activities, facilitating inclusive participation and interactive experiences.
➤Beneficiary Interaction: Built rapport with participants through games, clay sculpting, and direct engagement.
➤Skills Developed: Strengthened leadership, stakeholder management, event planning, empathy, teamwork, and adaptability.
